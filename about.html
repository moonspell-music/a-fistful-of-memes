Our Purpose

We believe that memes are beneficial in terms of helping Filipinos cope, fostering social bonds, simplifying complex topics, and calling out corruption or governance issues.

Thus, our purpose is to determine how exposed Filipinos are to political memes, how relatable they find them, what their thoughts are, and how these memes raise awareness about governance issues.
