Corruption remains as a major issue in the Philippines, causing it to weaken its trust from the general public (Watanabe, 2025).

Despite this, Filipinos often respond with resilience through humor and online trends, such as memes, to be able to express their political opinions and frustrations (Garcia, 2018) and not only as a form of entertainment (Balmores-Paulino, 2018).

By turning these issues into humorous content, people share collective sentiments and build connection.
